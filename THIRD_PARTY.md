# Third-Party Notices

## Bundled theme ports

The native themes in `themes/` were adapted from the theme collection in
[btop v1.4.7](https://github.com/aristocratos/btop/tree/v1.4.7/themes),
commit [`6e39144aaf5a6bc01b9f795010b0914431067183`](https://github.com/aristocratos/btop/commit/6e39144aaf5a6bc01b9f795010b0914431067183).
btop distributes these files under the Apache License 2.0. The files were
modified for amdtop's TOML schema, semantic UI roles, independent GPU/NPU
gradients, and compile-time embedding. The Apache-2.0 text is in `LICENSE`.

Many entries are ports of independently created color palettes rather than
designs originating in btop. Names are used descriptively and do not imply
endorsement. Original source links and contributor notices carried by btop
are retained in the native theme files and summarized below.
